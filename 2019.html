Rules

Group stage games are 25 minute halves with running clock

Playoff games are 30 minute halves with running clock

Group stage games can end in a tie

Playoff games ending in a tie go straight to PK shootout

Group stage tiebreaker(s):

  1. Head to head
  2. Goal difference
  3. Total goals scored
  4. Coin toss
